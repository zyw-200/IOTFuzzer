Scheduled Wireless On-Off Help

Scheduled Wireless on-off

Enable Scheduled Wireless to allow the scheduling of automatic ON/OFF of Radio.

    • Radio off schedule : The facility to enable Scheduled Wireless Feature for whatever day of week it is required for.
    • Radio ON time: To configure the time in hours and minutes when Radio is intended to get ON.
    • Radio OFF time: To configure the time in hours and minutes when Radio is intended to get OFF.
