Wireless On-Off Help

Wireless on-off

Enable Wireless On-Off to allow the scheduling of automatic ON/OFF of the Radio.

    • Radio off schedule : The facility to enable Wireless On-Off Feature for whatever day of week it is required.
    • Radio ON time: To configure the time in hours and minutes when the Radio is intended to be ON.
    • Radio OFF time: To configure the time in hours and minutes when the Radio is intended to be OFF.
